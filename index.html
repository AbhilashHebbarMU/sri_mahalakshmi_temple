About the Temple

Rooted in centuries of faith, our temple is a beacon for devotees seeking peace, blessings, and community. Daily poojas, seasonal festivals, and charitable initiatives keep the traditions vibrant.

Whether visiting for darshana, seva, or simply to find calm, you are welcome. Our priests and volunteers are always present to guide you.
